Dad's Favorite Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Make these delicious peanut butter cookies for Dad this Father's day!

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 87
  • Fat: 5g
  • Carbs: 10g
  • Protein: 2g

Ingredients

  • ½ cup shortening
  • ½ cup crunchy peanut butter
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, plus more as needed
  • ¾ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
