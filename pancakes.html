Double Whole Grain Pancakes

Description

Make these delicious peanut butter cookies for Dad this Father's day!

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 121
  • Fat: 4g
  • Carbs: 17g
  • Protein: 4g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 ⅓ cups dry milk powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 3 cups water
  • ¼ cup butter, melted
  • 3 tablespoons vinegar
